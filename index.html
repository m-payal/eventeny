Common questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need multiple tools to run my event? +
Most platforms focus on one thing — ticketing, or applications, or mapping. Eventeny puts all of it in one place. You get vendor management, volunteer tools, ticketing, sponsor management, and event mapping under a single login with no third-party stitching required.
How hard is it to migrate from another platform? +
Your onboarding manager walks you through data migration and setup. Most organizers are live within a week. The knowledge base and community center cover every step, and phone support is available when you need a human.
Are there hidden fees I should know about? +
No. The platform fee is $240 per month. Transaction fees are 3%. Setup is free. Features like vendor management, volunteer tools, and event mapping are all included.
What types of events does Eventeny support? +
Art festivals, food and drink events, community fairs, fandom conventions, music festivals, markets, pride events, and heritage festivals. If your event has vendors, volunteers, or sponsors, Eventeny was built for it.
What support is included? +
Every plan includes a dedicated onboarding manager, phone support, email support, access to the knowledge base, and community center resources. Large-scale events get a dedicated account manager at no extra cost.
